Agentforce Discovery — Online Survey

Copy/paste into your survey tool. Mark required questions and set question types as recommended.

Section A — Respondent details

  1. Your name (Short text) — optional
  2. Role / team (Dropdown) — Executive, Product, Operations, Sales, CS, End User, IT, Security, Data, Integration, Other
  3. Contact email (Short text) — optional
  4. How familiar are you with the current Agentforce initiative? (Single choice) — Not aware / Somewhat aware / Very familiar

Section B — Business goals (to be filled by sponsors & product)

  1. What is the primary business objective for Agentforce? (Multi-select) — Increase revenue; Reduce cost; Improve compliance; Improve customer experience; Enable scale; Other (text)
  2. Rank the following success metrics in order of importance (Ranking) — Conversion rate, Time-to-resolution, Cost per case, SLA attainment, NPS, Revenue per user, Other (text)
  3. Target launch timeframe (Single choice) — <3 months / 3–6 months / 6–12 months / >12 months

Section C — Users & Usage

  1. Which persona best describes you? (Single choice) — Frontline Agent; Manager; Admin; IT; Executive; Other (text)
  2. How many hours per day would you expect to spend in Agentforce? (Single choice) — <1 / 1–2 / 2–4 / 4–8 / >8
  3. Primary device (Single choice) — Desktop/Laptop / Tablet / Mobile / Mixed
  4. Tech comfort level (Single choice) — Low / Medium / High

Section D — Current pain points (open + scale)

  1. Rate these current pain areas from 1 (low) to 5 (high) (Matrix) — Too many manual steps; Slow systems; Missing data; Poor integrations; Hard to find reports; Lack of training
  2. Describe top 1–3 pain points you experience today (Long text)

Section E — Functional priorities (multi-choice & short)

  1. Which features are must-haves for MVP? (Multi-select, ask to pick up to 5) — Lead capture; Assignment/routing; Approvals; Notifications; Analytics dashboards; Native mobile; Offline support; Role-based access; Audit logs; Customizable workflows; Integrations (CRM/email/telephony)
  2. For each selected feature, explain the essential behavior or rule (Long text)

Section F — Integrations & Data

  1. Which systems do we need to connect at launch? (Multi-select) — Salesforce/CRM; ERP; Email; Telephony; Identity Provider (SSO); Data Warehouse; Other (text)
  2. Do you have sample data or API docs you can share? (Single choice) — Yes / No (If yes, provide upload link or contact)

Section G — Non-functional & Security

  1. What uptime/availability is required? (Single choice) — 99% / 99.5% / 99.9% / 99.99%
  2. Any data residency or regulatory constraints? (Long text)
  3. Authentication required (Multi-select) — SSO/SAML; OAuth; Local accounts; MFA; Other

Section H — Reporting & Analytics

  1. Which KPIs must be on dashboards at launch? (Multi-select) — Conversion rate; SLA compliance; Avg handle time; Pipeline value; Team performance; Other (text)
  2. Do you need self-service reporting? (Single choice) — Yes / No / Maybe

Section I — Training & Adoption

  1. What training format would be most useful? (Multi-select) — Live workshops; Recorded videos; Quick reference guides; In-app help; Shadowing
  2. What level of customization do users need for their interface? (Single choice) — None / Minor (reorder widgets) / Moderate (custom fields, layouts) / Heavy (workflow builder)

Section J — Final

  1. Biggest risk or concern (Long text)
  2. Anything else we should know? (Long text)
  3. Would you be willing to participate in follow-up sessions? (Single choice) — Yes / No

Survey tips

  • Make sections conditional (e.g., show business questions only to Executives).
  • Limit required fields to essential items to maximize completion.
  • Attach a sample data upload form or provide clear instructions for sending CSV/API docs.

End of Survey template.
